Aam Lassi Recipe

Description

Aam Lassi is a sweet dairy drink combined with mango pulp. It originates from India and consumed there heavily in the summers

Ingredients

  • 1 cups mangos - peeled, seeded, chopped, and chilled
  • 1 cups plain yogurt
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom

Steps

  1. Combine mangos, yogurt, milk, cream, sugar, and cardamom in a blender; blend until smooth and frothy.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
